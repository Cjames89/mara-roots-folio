
# Blockchain Professional Portfolio Website

A dark, techy personal portfolio for a blockchain researcher and Revenue Operations professional at MARA.

---

## Design Direction

**Dark & Techy Aesthetic**
- Deep dark backgrounds (#0a0a0f) with subtle gradients
- Neon accent colors (electric blue/cyan for blockchain vibes)
- Geometric patterns and subtle grid overlays
- Sleek, modern typography with monospace accents for tech credibility
- Smooth scroll animations and hover effects throughout

---

## Pages & Sections

### 1. Hero Section
- Full-screen hero with animated gradient background
- Professional headline and brief tagline
- Animated typing effect or subtle particle animation
- Call-to-action buttons (View Work, Download Resume)
- Social links (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, GitHub)

### 2. About Section
- Professional bio highlighting blockchain expertise and RevOps experience
- Photo placeholder with stylized frame/glow effect
- Skills visualization (tech stack, tools, certifications)
- Current role at MARA featured prominently

### 3. Experience Timeline
- Vertical timeline with animated reveal on scroll
- MARA - Revenue Operations role (current)
- Previous blockchain/research positions
- Key achievements and responsibilities for each role
- Company logos and dates

### 4. Research & Publications
- Card grid showcasing research papers and articles
- Filter by category (blockchain, RevOps, general)
- Each card shows title, abstract preview, publication date
- Links to external publications

### 5. Projects Showcase
- Featured projects with image previews
- Tech stack tags for each project
- Problem/solution/impact descriptions
- Live demo links and GitHub repos where applicable

### 6. Blog Section (Substack Integration)
- Fetches your latest posts from Substack RSS feed
- Displays post cards with title, excerpt, date
- "Read on Substack" links to full articles
- Subscribe button linking to your Substack page
- Configurable Substack URL (you just enter your Substack username)

### 7. Contact Section
- Social media links with hover animations
- Email link (mailto:)
- LinkedIn, Twitter/X, GitHub icons
- Resume download button (PDF)

---

## Key Features

**Smooth Animations**
- Scroll-triggered fade-in and slide animations
- Hover effects on cards and buttons
- Animated background elements
- Subtle particle/grid effects

**Resume Download**
- Prominent download button in hero and contact sections
- Upload your PDF and it downloads directly

**Substack Integration**
- Enter your Substack URL once
- Automatically pulls in your latest posts
- No content management needed - write on Substack, it appears here

---

## Technical Approach

- Fully responsive design (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- SEO-optimized with proper meta tags
- No backend required - all client-side
- RSS parsing for Substack content
- Clean, maintainable code structure
